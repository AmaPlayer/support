Frequently Asked Questions

What is AmaPlayer?

AmaPlayer is India's sports talent discovery platform where athletes can create profiles, upload performance videos, earn scout scores, and get discovered by verified scouts, coaches, and organizations.

Is AmaPlayer free for athletes?

Yes! Creating an athlete profile, uploading videos, and participating in events is completely free. AmaPlayer is built to give every athlete an equal chance to be discovered.

How does the Scout Score work?

The Scout Score is calculated based on your consistency, video uploads, community engagement, and verified achievements. It rewards athletes who show up and put in the work daily — not just highlight reels. Learn more about Scout Scores.

How can scouts and organizations find athletes?

Scouts and organizations can browse athlete profiles filtered by sport, location, and scout score. They can watch performance videos, review achievements, and contact athletes directly through the app. Learn more.

How do I register my organization or academy?

Download the AmaPlayer app, register as an organization, and complete your profile with your facilities, sports offered, and programs. Once verified, you'll be listed on our platform. Learn more about organizations.

How do I report a bug or issue?

Use the contact form below to report any bugs or issues. Please include as much detail as possible — your device, what you were doing, and screenshots if possible. We'll investigate and respond within 24 hours.

Is my data safe on AmaPlayer?

Yes. We take data privacy seriously. Your personal information is encrypted and never shared without your consent. Only verified scouts and organizations can view athlete profiles. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
